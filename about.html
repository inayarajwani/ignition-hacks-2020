Our Story!

Are you visually impaired? If the answers is yes, check out our application!

We are four pogrammers working on an application to provide a pleasing and 
practical experience for the disabled, especially the visually impaired. 
Through our pogram you can select a paragraph and through text to speech 
technology, it will read it out loud. With this, we hope to make ground-breaking 
leap into helping the disabled to learn.
